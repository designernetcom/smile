Dental Implants

Dental Implants are a permanent solution for replacing missing teeth, offering unmatched stability and a natural appearance. We specialize in implant dentistry, providing patients with durable and aesthetically pleasing replacements for lost teeth. Dental implants consist of a titanium post that acts as a root, topped with a crown that looks and functions like a natural tooth. They are ideal for those looking for a long-lasting solution to restore their smile and improve oral health.

Benefits

  • Permanent Solution

    Dental implants provide a lifelong replacement for missing teeth.

  • Natural Appearance

    Implants look and feel like real teeth, blending seamlessly with your smile.

  • Improved Oral Health

    Implants help preserve jawbone structure and prevent bone loss.

  • Enhanced Functionality

    Allows for normal chewing and speaking, restoring full mouth function.

Types of Implants

Single Tooth Implant

Replaces one missing tooth with a single implant and crown


Multiple Tooth Implants

Replaces several missing teeth with individual implants or implant-supported bridges.

Full Mouth Reconstruction

Replaces all teeth in the upper or lower jaw with implant-supported dentures


Procedure

  • Consultation and Planning

    Detailed examination and discussion of the best treatment options.

  • Implant Placement

    Surgical insertion of the titanium post into the jawbone.

  • Healing Period

    Allowing time for the implant to integrate with the bone.

  • Crown Placement

    Attaching the custom-made crown to the implant post.

FAQ

The procedure is done under anesthesia, and most patients experience minimal discomfort

With proper care, implants can last a lifetime.

Most people with healthy gums and sufficient bone structure are good candidates.
